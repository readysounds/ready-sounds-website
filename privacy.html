Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Ready Sounds ("we," "us," or "our") operates readysounds.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your plaintext password)
  • Account creation date

Billing Information

When you purchase a subscription or individual license, we collect:

  • Name and billing address (processed by Stripe)
  • Payment method details (processed and stored by Stripe — we never store card numbers)
  • Transaction history and subscription status

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the site:

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Tracks browsed, previewed, and downloaded
  • IP address and general geographic region
  • Browser type and device information
  • Referring URLs

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To provide access to licensed music downloads
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, subscription confirmations, renewal reminders)
  • To respond to support and licensing inquiries
  • To improve the site and catalog based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising targeting or share it with data brokers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate Ready Sounds. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Supabase

Handles user authentication and stores account data (email, subscription status, download history). Data is hosted on servers in the United States. Supabase Privacy Policy →

Stripe

Processes all payments and manages subscription billing. Stripe handles and stores your payment card details — we never have access to your full card number. Stripe Privacy Policy →

Cloudflare R2

Stores the music files available for download. Download requests are authenticated and use short-lived signed URLs — files are not publicly accessible. Cloudflare Privacy Policy →

Google Analytics 4

Collects anonymized usage data (page views, session duration, traffic sources) to help us understand how people use the site. IP addresses are anonymized. Google Privacy Policy →

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Keeping you logged in to your account (session cookies via Supabase)
  • Analytics: Understanding site usage via Google Analytics 4
  • Payments: Stripe may set cookies during the checkout process

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels for ad retargeting.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes (e.g., transaction records, which are kept for 7 years per standard accounting requirements).

Analytics data collected by Google Analytics is retained for 14 months.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (you can use browser-level settings or extensions to block Google Analytics)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Ready Sounds is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify active subscribers by email.

Questions or Requests

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, contact us at:
