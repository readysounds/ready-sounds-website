Frequently Asked Questions

What can I use these tracks for?

Individual plan: YouTube videos, podcasts, social media content, wedding films, personal projects. Business plan: All of the above plus client work, TV/film, advertising, and commercial projects.

Do I need to credit the artist?

No attribution required, but it's always appreciated! You're free to use the music without crediting Buck Moon or Ready Sounds.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You retain the license to use any tracks you downloaded while subscribed. You just won't be able to download new tracks until you resubscribe.

Can I download stems and alternate versions?

Yes! Every track includes stems (individual instrument tracks) and alternate versions like intros, breakdowns, and beats. Perfect for customizing to your content.

How is this different from Epidemic Sound or Artlist?

Ready Sounds is run by an actual music producer (Buck Moon), not a corporation. You're licensing directly from the artist at fair prices. Plus, we focus on quality over quantity‚Äîcurated tracks, not a bloated library.
